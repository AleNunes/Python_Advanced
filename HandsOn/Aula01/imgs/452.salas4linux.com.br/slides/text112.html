Apache SSL (https) – Ativando SSL no Site da Dexter:

  • Gerar Chave Privada:

    • 1# openssl genrsa -out /etc/ssl/dexter.key 1024

  • Gerar Requisição (CSR - Certificate Signing Request):

    • 2# openssl req -new -key /etc/ssl/dexter.key -out /etc/ssl/dexter.csr

  • Auto Assinar o Certificado:

    • 3# openssl x509 -req -days 365 -in /etc/ssl/dexter.csr -signkey /etc/ssl/dexter.key -out /etc/ssl/dexter.crt
